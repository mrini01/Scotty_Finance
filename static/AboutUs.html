Our mission

College students often struggle with tight budgets and unforeseen expenses. The goal of creating a budgeting website is to make money management a breeze. Scotty finance aims to simplify money management, creating a platform tailored specifically for students. Unlike other platforms, it allows for personalized customization to perfectly meet each student’s unique needs. It’s designed not only to monitor expenses, but also provide students with the tools to strategize their financial plans throughout their college journey. Now, more than ever with the economic strains of inflation and instability, Scotty Finance aims to be the best budgeting platform for students.
